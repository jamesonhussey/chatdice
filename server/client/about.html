Welcome to ChatDice - your gateway to spontaneous conversations with people from around the world!

What is ChatDice?

ChatDice is a free, anonymous chat platform that connects you with random people instantly. Think of it as rolling the dice on who you'll meet next - you never know who you'll be matched with or what interesting conversations await!

How It Works

1-on-1 Chat: Get matched with one random person for a private conversation. Perfect for making new friends or having deep discussions.

Group Chat: Join a lively group conversation with 9 other people. Great for diverse perspectives and fun group dynamics.

Smart Queue System: Don't worry if no one's available right now - we'll keep you in the queue for up to an hour and notify you the moment someone else is looking to chat!

Our Mission

We believe in the power of random connections. In a world where we often stick to our existing social circles, ChatDice breaks down barriers and creates opportunities for unexpected, meaningful conversations.

Safety & Privacy

Your safety is our priority:

  • All chats are anonymous - no account required
  • Messages are stored for 30 days for safety/moderation purposes, then automatically deleted
  • Report button available in every chat
  • Rate limiting prevents spam
  • We take abuse reports seriously

Community Guidelines

ChatDice is a place for respectful, genuine conversations. We don't tolerate:

  • Harassment, hate speech, or bullying
  • Sharing of illegal content
  • Spam or advertising
  • Sexual content or solicitation

Violations will result in immediate removal from the platform and may be reported to authorities if necessary.
